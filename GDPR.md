# 🛡️ GDPR Compliance Statement

## 1. Introduction

This document describes how the Crossword App complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

---

## 2. Data Collection

This application does **not collect or process personal data** such as:

* Names
* Emails
* IP addresses (stored)
* Location data

---

## 3. Cookies Usage

The application uses cookies to:

* Maintain user preferences
* Improve usability

Cookies are only activated after user consent.

---

## 4. User Consent

A cookie consent popup is displayed when the user first visits the application.

Users can:

* Accept cookies
* Decline cookies

This follows the **opt-in requirement** of GDPR.

---

## 5. Data Storage

No personal data is stored on servers or databases.

---

## 6. Third-party Services

Some third-party libraries (npm packages) may be used.
Their licenses are listed in:

```id="jz71n7"
licenses.txt
```

---

## 7. User Rights

Under GDPR, users have the right to:

* Be informed
* Refuse cookies
* Withdraw consent

---

## 8. Security

Since no personal data is stored, risks related to data breaches are minimal.

---

## 9. Changes

This document may be updated without prior notice.

---

## 10. Conclusion

The application follows GDPR principles:

* Data minimization
* Transparency
* User consent
